We need your help to build HistoryTracks. How? Become a HistoryTracks Ambassador and help us tag and narrate interesting historical sites in your neighborhood or according to your interests (e.g. Jazz History Sites, Civil War Sites, etc.) Or even if you have just one great story to tell us, we want to hear about it. For inclusion into HistoryTracks, a historical story must meet the following criteria: 1.) It must be true! (…or perceived to be true) 2.) It must be historically relevant! (…not where you had lunch) 3.) It must be educational! (…give us some historical context…) 4.) It must be interesting/entertaining! (…not boring) You can reach us at: stories@historytracks.com.
